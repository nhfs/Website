 The Grand Marais Art Colony and North House Folk School are excited to announce their new, collaborative effort, The North Shore Series. Join us for a
year-long lineup of courses that reflect the two organizations’ mutual
commitment to nurturing the arts on the North Shore.

Courses will introduce students to both learning institutions
and will be cross-listed in each organization’s catalog and website.

Courses in the North Shore Series:

Offered by North House Folk School
 - Woodblock Printing - March 4-6
 - Romancing the Stone: Intro to Inuit Soapstone Carving - March 16-18
 - Inuit Soapstone Carving: Speaking through Sculpture - March 20-21
 - Low-Tech Clay Forming & Firing: Low & Slow - Sept 9-11
 - Exploration of Wheel Throwing and Surface Treatments - Sept 16-17

Offered by The Grand Marais Art Colony
 - Inuit Stonecut Prints - March 16-17
 - Inuit Stencil Prints - March 20-21
 - Eco-Printing: Wearable Art - MON XX
 - North Shore Pebble Mosaic - Aug 20-21
