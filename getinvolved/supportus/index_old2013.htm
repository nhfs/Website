Giving Opportunities

 Thank you for your interest in North House Folk School and our educational efforts!  North House is a 501-c3 educational nonprofit and thrives thanks to the purposeful and generous involvement of many friends and supports!

There are many great ways to support North House.
Three avenues for giving today:

1. Donate Now - Become a Member

• Support If you value of North House we need your support! Every gift makes a difference, BOTH by providing essential operating support AND by affirming the North House mission! Donate Now!

• Sharpen the Saw: In 2013 North House launched a three-year initiative to improve classroom resources: lights to lathes, hand tools to workbenches. A new instructor retreat, apprenticeship program and outreach efforts will set the stage for future chapters of craft education. Donate Now!

Questions? Call 218-387-9762

2. Sustain The Dream

Become a North House Sustaining Member and help sustain the dream.
Monthly scheduled contributions are automatically withdrawn from your credit card during the first week of every month. Choose between a single year or an automatically renewable contribution. Solid, consistent support to sustain the dream.
Becoming a Sustaining Member - click here.

3. Step Forward in Other Creative Ways:

In-Kind resources, targeted Endowment Fund contributions,
Memorial gifts and gifts of stock/securities are all accepted by North House. Questions or other ideas? Please call us anytime at 218-387-9762
