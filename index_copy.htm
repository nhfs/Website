   Timber frames to wooden boats,  basketry to woodcarving, fiber arts to woodworking - celebrating traditional northern crafts and the joy of learning is what North House is all about.  Are you ready to "Journey North" and be part of a class, course or workshop?  We hope so.

   There is tremendous joy and satisfaction in hands-on learning, a power in reconnecting to the North and its many stories. We hope to see you on campus soon!
