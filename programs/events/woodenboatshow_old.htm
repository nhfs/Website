Keynote Presentation "Boats & Stories of Isle Royale" | Sunday at Noon
with Howard Sivertson, Gene Skadberg and Tim Cochrane
Join North Shore residents Howard Sivertson and Gene Skadberg, accompanied by historian Tim Cochrane, as they share their memories of life on Isle Royale. Growing up in a maritime culture in which boats were cars, the lake was the road and fish were (nearly always) on the menu, Howard and Gene’s experiences are a window into a world centered around boats.

Featured Instructor Douglas Brooks
Douglas is a boatbuilder, writer and researcher. He has researched traditional Japanese boat building for over two decades and has apprenticed with five boatbuilders in Japan. Sign up for his lofting class here. To learn more about his research, please visit: www.douglasbrooksboatbuilding.com.
