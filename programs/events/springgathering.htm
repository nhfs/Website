TWIN CITIES
SPRING GATHERING
 May 17, 2014
 

Craft Workshops | Wood-Fired Pizza
Michael Johnson Concert


Feeling a little stir crazy from this long, chilly winter? Spring fever is just around the corner, and North House wants to celebrate the warmth of a new season by coming to YOU! On Saturday, May 17, we’ll host our third annual Twin Cities Spring Gathering – the chance to celebrate our growing community “down south” and raise funds to help carry out our work. Unique mini-courses, tasty wood-fired pizza, and rockin’ music by Billboard-charting singer/songwriter Michael Johnson – you do not want to miss the fun! Held at Clifton Hollow Golf Course in River Falls, WI, let’s connect with old friends but also welcome new ones. Invite someone along and get your North House fix until your next visit to the big lake!

What’s included in the day? Click here to see the full schedule
  - Full- and half-day mini courses
  - PB&J Lunch
  - Special Craft Demos
  - Social Hour with live music, games and more
  - Wood-fired Pizza Dinner
  - Musical Performance by Minnesota's beloved Michael Johnson 
Coffee and lemonade available for a modest price throughout the day. Beer and liquor available at on-site bar in the evening.
