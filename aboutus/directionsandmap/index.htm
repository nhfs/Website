DIRECTIONS & MAP

Grand Marais ("grand muh-ray") is located in the Arrowhead region of northeastern Minnesota on scenic Highway 61, about two hours north of Duluth, Minnesota, on the shore of Lake Superior. The journey along the North Shore is beautiful, including many rocky points, stunning vistas and pebbled beaches.   

North House Folk School's campus sits on the Grand Marais harbor.  It can be easily located at the intersection of Highway 61 and 5th Avenue West.  The entrance to the campus is on the lake side of the road, immediately adjacent to the yellow & blue North House sign.  Students and visitors are encouraged to check in at the school office/store in the "blue building" as they arrive.

Want to carpool to North House? Try North Shore Rideshare.
Shuttle Service available from Duluth via Superior Shuttle
